About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Smart Structures Lab at the University of British Columbia (UBC), Vancouver, Canada. My current research focuses on modular structures, spanning modular manufacturing and assembly, modular structural performance assessment and enhancement under seismic loads, and the safety of modular connections, using numerical analysis, shake table testing, robotics, and AI-powered solutions to enhance modular structural performance and construction efficiency.
