Haopeng Duan

Personal Information

I am a second-year MPhil student at the High-Speed Railway Engineering Structure Seismic Research Institute. My research interests include finite element simulation, performance-based earthquake engineering and seismic analysis. Currently, my research is centered on the effects of near-fault hanging wall effect and velocity-pulse effect on seismic damage of high-speed railway long-span steel truss arch bridge-track system. I acquired my B.S. in Civil Engineering from the Northeast Agricultural University in June 2022. I began my postgraduate studies at the Central South University in September 2022. I am scheduled to graduate in June 2025.
I am privileged to be advised by Dr. Liqiang Jiang and Prof. Lizhong Jiang.
