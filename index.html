Personal Information

I am a second-year postgraduate student at the High-Speed Railway Engineering Structure Seismic Research Institute. My research interests include finite element (FE) simulation and seismic analysis, and also involve earthquake engineering and machine learning. Currently, my academic research focuses on investigating the effects of near-fault hanging wall effect and velocity-pulse effect on an high-speed railway long-span steel truss arch bridge. I entered Northeast Agricultural University (NEAU) to study civil engineering (spatial reticulated shell structure) in 2018 and obtained a Bachelor's degree in civil engineering in 2022. Later, in the same year, I entered Central South University (CSU) to study for a Master's degree in structural engineering. I will graduate in June 2025.

I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Liqiang Jiang and Prof. Lizhong Jiang.
