About Me

I am a third-year master student at Central South University, China, specializing in structural and earthquake engineering. My current research focuses on the seismic performance and resilience of high-speed railway deck arch bridges under near-fault ground motions. I have a strong interest in structural nonlinear numerical simulation, shaking table testing, and AI-driven solutions in civil engineering. I earned my bachelor's degree in civil engineering from Northeast Agricultural University in 2022 and am expected to obtain my master's degree from Central South University in 2025.
