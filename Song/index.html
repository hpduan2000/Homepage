Guofeng Song

Personal Information

I am a second-year postgraduate student at the High-Speed Railway Engineering Structure Seismic Research Institute. My research interests include finite element (FE) simulation, seismic analysis, performance-based earthquake engineering and seismic design. Currently, my research project is centered on the seismic behavior of HSR long-span steel truss arch bridge in near-fault zones. I acquired a B.S. in Civil Engineering from Northeast Agricultural University (NEAU) in June 2022. I began my graduate studies at the Central South University (CSU) in September 2022. I am scheduled to graduate in June 2025.
I am privileged to be advised by Dr. Liqiang Jiang and Prof. Lizhong Jiang.
My guys: Ximing Zheng, Haopeng Duan
